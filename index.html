Donghee Choi, Ph.D. (최동희)

iamdongheechoi@gmail.com
Donghee Choi, Ph.D., is a Research Associate at [Imperial College London](https://profiles.imperial.ac.uk/donghee.choi/) since November 2023, supervised by [Joram M. Posma](https://www.imperial.ac.uk/people/j.posma11). He earned his Ph.D. in Computer Science from [Korea University](https://korea.edu) in August 2023, where he was advised by [Professor Jaewoo Kang](https://dmis.korea.ac.kr).
He also holds an M.S. in Bioinformatics and a B.S. in Computer Science from Korea University, both supervised by [Professor Jaewoo Kang](https://dmis.korea.ac.kr). His research interests span AI, Bioinformatics, and Computational Science, with a focus on applications of AI in gastronomy, human-computer interaction, and knowledge representation.
Donghee has extensive experience in academia and industry. He interned at [Sony AI](https://ai.sony) on their Gastronomy AI project, co-founded and worked with startups like LYZE, Konolabs, and Opinion8, and contributed to diverse projects involving AI applications in various domains. He has also published extensively in international conferences and journals, showcasing his contributions to the intersection of AI and real-world problems.
Donghee is currently exploring fellowship opportunities and academic collaborations in the UK and beyond. He is passionate about advancing research at the nexus of AI and multidisciplinary applications.
